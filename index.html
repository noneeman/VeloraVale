Our story

The long way around to the same table

We found each other at a friend’s small dinner—two seats placed a little too close, a shared joke about the bread basket, and a walk afterward that turned into hours. What began as easy conversation became something quieter and deeper: two people who like the unglamorous parts of life as much as the beautiful ones.

Vale asked Velora on an ordinary Sunday, beside the garden they had planted together. There were no drones or flash mobs—only relief, laughter, and the sense that the answer had been forming for a long time. We are marrying in the Hudson Valley because it is where we breathe a little slower; we cannot wait to share that feeling with you.
